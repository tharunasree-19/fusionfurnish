What is your delivery policy?

We offer free delivery within city limits for orders above ₹15,000. For other areas, delivery charges start at ₹999 depending on the distance and size of the furniture.

Do you offer warranty on your furniture?

Yes, all our furniture comes with a 1-year warranty against manufacturing defects. Premium items have extended warranties of up to 3 years.

Can I return or exchange furniture?

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery if the item is in its original condition. Custom-made furniture is non-returnable. Exchange is possible subject to availability.

How do I care for my furniture?

Different materials require different care. We provide care instructions with each purchase. Generally, avoid direct sunlight, use coasters for hot items, and dust regularly with a soft cloth.

Do you offer EMI options?

Yes, we offer No-Cost EMI options on selected bank credit cards for purchases above ₹10,000. EMI tenure ranges from 3 to 12 months.
